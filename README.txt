Instructions: 
Complete the tutorial then follow the campaign or survive any way you can find.

Controls:
WASD - Movement buttons
Left Mouse - Use equipped weapon/tool
E - Interact with the world

Minigame Controls:

Fishing game - Left click to lower line to catch and collect fish, beware of bad fish and obstacles that may reduce your health while trying to collect good fish to eat to replenish health

Pyramid platformer - A,D or Left and Right arrow keys for movement, and space jump. Climb to the top of the pyramid
